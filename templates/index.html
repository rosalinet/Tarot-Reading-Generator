Welcome to the Tarot Reading Generator!

Each spread can be SITUATIONAL or GENERAL. For a situational reading, please think of a particular situation in your life carefully before choosing your spread. When ready, select which spread you'd like to generate!

(You can read about each type of spread below the buttons!)






About the Different Spreads

─── ･ ｡ﾟ☆: *.☽ The 'Past, Present, and Future' Spread .* :☆ﾟ. ───

✧ The first card represents elements from the past that AFFECT your current situation.
✧ The second card describes the current STATUS of the situation.
✧ The third card gives insight into a POSSIBLE OUTCOME of the situation.

─── ･ ｡ﾟ☆: *.☽ The 'Mind, Body, and Soul' Spread .* :☆ﾟ. ───

✧ The first card represents your current logic, reasoning, mental health, and rationality.
✧ The second card represents your current health, fitness, sexuality, and activity.
✧ The third card represents your current emotions, creativity, spirituality, and love.

─── ･ ｡ﾟ☆: *.☽ The 'Strengths, Weaknesses, and Advice' Spread .* :☆ﾟ. ───

✧ The first card highlights your strengths.
✧ The second card highlights your weaknesses.
✧ The third card suggests a possible action or approach.

─── ･ ｡ﾟ☆: *.☽ The 'You, Your Partner, and Your Relationship' Spread (Version 1) .* :☆ﾟ. ───

✧ The first card describes what you want in the relationship.
✧ The second card describes what your partner wants in the relationship.
✧ The third card describes where the relationship could potentially be heading.

─── ･ ｡ﾟ☆: *.☽ The 'You, Your Partner, and Your Relationship' Spread (Version 2) .* :☆ﾟ. ───

✧ The first card describes what brings you both together.
✧ The second card describes what pulls you both apart.
✧ The third card describes what needs your attention.
